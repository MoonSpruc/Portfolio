My story

Hi there, my name is Andrii, I am 18 years old.

My journey into front-end development started with a simple interest. One day I saw an offer on the Internet to take a trial course from an IT company, where I was offered to learn the basics of HTML and CSS by creating my first website.

That was my first step into the world of web development.


And i loved it. Since then, I've gradually delved deeper into learning the technologies and tools needed to create modern web applications. With each new project, I discovered something new that motivated me to move forward.


This process of learning and creating my own websites became my passion and led me to choose front-end development as my main focus.
